My Values

Here's a random list of things that I believe in:

  • Dark Mode should be everywhere.
  • The Amazon website and app is an appalling tragedy when it comes to its UI design.
  • Open Source software is amazing and its development and adoption should be encouraged by everyone.
  • However, some things are worth paying for. Including great software.
  • Apps should be designed and developed so that they fit in with the platform that they're being developed on. E.g. iOS apps should conform to the Human Interface Guidelines and Google apps should use material design.
  • Apps should be updated to take advantage of new OS updates and features as soon as possible.
  • Jony Ive is the greatest designer ever to grace this earth. 🌍
  • Facebook pursued profit at the expense of their user experience, privacy and design.
  • Innocent until proven guilty. Unfortunately this isn't always the case anymore.
