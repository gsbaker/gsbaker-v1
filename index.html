Hey there 👋🏼 I’m an engineering intern at FreeAgent, a computer science undergraduate at the University of Birmingham, and an amateur photographer.

I've been interested in computers, technology, programming — and the impact of all those things on humans and society since a young age.

I believe that computers should just 'fit in' and enable us to do great things that otherwise wouldn't be possible or easy.

I am currently particularly interested in our relationship with social media from an HCI perspective — and whether we can create a healthier one.

Photography is a growing passion of mine.

I am a big fan of simple compositions and bold colours.

I love taking landscapes, candid photographs at events, and natural looking portraits.

If you are interested in my work as a photographer, please look at my gallery and send me an email.
